My name is Pratim Chowdhary and I am a Dartmouth Computer Science and Mathematics student from Jericho, Long Island. I am a first generation immigrant and come from Indian heritage. Ever since middle school I have been working on programming projects, learning everything from full stack programming to advanced machine learning along the way. As an extension of my passion for computer science, I've picked up a strong knowledge of mathematics along the way, with everything from probability theory to multivariable calculus. In my free time I love playing sports, and was part of the Jericho High School Varisty Wrestling and Track and Field teams. I hope to one day create my own startup and use my passion as a way to advance the progress of the underprivelaged around the world.
